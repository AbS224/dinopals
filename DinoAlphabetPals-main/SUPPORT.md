# Support Policy

This project is community-supported on a best-effort basis.

- No SLA: We do not guarantee response times for issues or pull requests.
- Availability: Maintainers may not be able to respond promptly or at all.
- Security: For vulnerabilities, use private advisories per `SECURITY.md`.
- Self-Help: Feel free to fork, experiment, and adapt to your needs under the MIT License.
- Contributions: PRs are welcome and reviewed as time permits.
